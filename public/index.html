// Scoring signals
Domain Age 0 – 30 pts

WHOIS creation date. Domains registered years ago signal operational permanence. A domain created last week scores near zero regardless of other factors.

TLD Risk 0 – 20 pts

.com / .org / .io / .ai / .dev score highest. Known high-risk TLDs (.tk, .xyz, .click, .ga) score zero — they're disproportionately used in fraud and phishing.

DNS Presence 0 – 30 pts

Does the domain actually resolve? An A record confirms something is running. MX records confirm a real operational presence with email infrastructure.

Registrar 10 – 20 pts

Known established registrars (GoDaddy, Cloudflare, MarkMonitor, Google, Namecheap, CSC) signal legitimate intent. Unknown or obscure registrars score lower.
